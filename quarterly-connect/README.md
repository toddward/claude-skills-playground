A strategic AI partner that guides employees through comprehensive quarterly performance reflections, helping craft insightful narratives that align with company values and career development goals.

## What Is This Skill?

The Quarterly Connection Assistant transforms the often-daunting task of quarterly performance reviews into a structured, thoughtful conversation. It acts as your strategic partner, helping you reflect on achievements, articulate challenges, and plan future growth while ensuring alignment with your company's specific core values and culture.

## How It Works

### Phase 1: Company Context (Automatic)
1. You provide your company name
2. The skill searches for your company's core values and mission statement
3. Confirms the values with you for accuracy
4. Stores these values to use throughout the conversation

### Phase 2: Data Collection (Guided)
The skill walks you through:
- **Top 3 Wins**: Major achievements and their business impact
- **Strengths**: Areas where you excelled
- **Challenges**: What didn't go as planned and lessons learned
- **Next Quarter Focus**: Priorities and goals aligned with team/company objectives
- **Career Aspirations**: Role evolution and growth opportunities
- **Feedback Integration**: How you've incorporated feedback received

At each step, the skill asks clarifying questions to help you:
- Identify measurable outcomes
- Connect work to stakeholder impact
- Align achievements with company values
- Provide concrete examples
- Surface patterns and trends

### Phase 3: Report Generation (Automated)
When you're ready, trigger report generation with phrases like:
- "Generate my quarterly connect"
- "Create the report for Q3"
- "Build my quarterly connection summary"

The skill produces a formatted report with:
- 8 comprehensive sections
- Explicit value alignment callouts
- Measurable outcomes and impact
- Professional formatting ready to share

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I use this for companies without public values?**
A: Yes! The skill has a fallback to common organizational values (Collaboration, Excellence, Accountability, Innovation, Customer Focus) if web search doesn't yield results.

**Q: How long does the process take?**
A: Typically 30-45 minutes for a complete quarterly reflection, less if you have notes prepared.

**Q: Can I generate reports for past quarters?**
A: Yes! Just specify the quarter (e.g., "generate my Q2 report") and the skill will filter by those date ranges.

**Q: What if my company uses different quarter dates?**
A: The skill uses standard quarters (Q1: Jan-Mar, Q2: Apr-Jun, Q3: Jul-Sep, Q4: Oct-Dec). You can still use it and manually adjust date references if needed.

**Q: Can I edit the output after generation?**
A: Absolutely! The generated markdown can be edited directly, or you can ask the skill to refine specific sections.

**Q: Is this only for tech roles?**
A: Not at all! The skill works for any role‚Äîsales, marketing, operations, design, etc. The structure adapts to your specific contributions.
